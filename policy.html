Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy and maintaining transparency in how information is handled.

Last Updated: June 2026

Introduction

Lalajuristbyrå AB ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our website visitors, clients, and users of our mobile applications. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, and the rights available to users regarding their personal information.

By accessing our website or using our applications, you acknowledge and accept the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Website Privacy Policy

This section applies to visitors of lalajuristbyraonline.store.

  • We collect personal information only when voluntarily provided by users.
  • Information submitted through contact forms or email communications is used solely to respond to inquiries and provide requested assistance.
  • We do not sell, rent, trade, or share personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.
  • Basic technical information such as browser type, operating system, and server logs may be processed for security and operational purposes.
  • Essential cookies may be used to maintain website functionality and security.

Information We Process

Depending on your interaction with our services, we may process:

  • Contact details voluntarily provided by you.
  • Communications sent through email or contact forms.
  • Technical and diagnostic information required for website security and maintenance.

We do not intentionally collect sensitive personal information unless required to provide requested legal services.

Mobile Applications Privacy Policy

This section applies to all mobile applications published by Lalajuristbyrå AB.

  • No Personal Data Collection – Our applications do not collect, store, transmit, or sell personal information.
  • No External Servers – Application data is not transmitted to company servers or third-party databases.
  • No Analytics or Tracking – We do not use analytics, advertising SDKs, tracking technologies, or behavioral profiling tools.
  • No Advertising – Our applications do not display personalized advertisements.
  • Offline Functionality – Applications are designed to operate without requiring an internet connection.
  • Local Storage Only – Any information created within the application remains stored locally on the user's device.
  • Privacy by Design – Privacy considerations are incorporated into the design and operation of our applications from the outset.

Data Security

We implement reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards designed to protect information against unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, misuse, or destruction.

While no online system can guarantee absolute security, we continuously strive to maintain appropriate protection measures.

Your Privacy Rights

Subject to applicable laws, users may have the right to:

  • Request access to personal information.
  • Request correction of inaccurate information.
  • Request deletion of personal information where applicable.
  • Object to certain processing activities.
  • Request information regarding how data is processed.

Data Retention

Information voluntarily provided through communications will be retained only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, or maintain business records.

Third-Party Services

Our website or applications may contain links to third-party websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices, content, or security of external websites. Users are encouraged to review the privacy policies of any third-party services they access.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect legal, operational, or regulatory changes.

Any updates will be published on this page together with the revised "Last Updated" date.
