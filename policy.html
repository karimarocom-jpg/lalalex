Privacy Policy

Protecting your privacy and maintaining transparency in how information is handled.

Last Updated: June 2026

Introduction

Lalajuristbyrå AB ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting the privacy and security of our website visitors, clients, and users of our mobile applications. This Privacy Policy explains what information may be collected, how it is used, and the rights available to users regarding their personal information.

By accessing our website or using our applications, you acknowledge and accept the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Website Privacy Policy

This section applies to visitors of lalajuristbyraonline.store.

  • We collect personal information only when voluntarily provided by users.
  • Information submitted through contact forms or email communications is used solely to respond to inquiries and provide requested assistance.
  • We do not sell, rent, trade, or share personal information with third parties for advertising or marketing purposes.
  • Basic technical information such as browser type, operating system, and server logs may be processed for security and operational purposes.
  • Essential cookies may be used to maintain website functionality and security.

Mobile Applications Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy applies to all applications (“the Apps”) developed and published by Lalajuristbyrå AB.

The Apps are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use any of our Apps.

Information Collection

  • Our Apps do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information.
  • No user registration is required, and the Apps do not collect: Names, Email addresses, Phone numbers, Location data, Device identifiers, Contacts, Photos or media files, Financial information.

Local Storage

  • Some Apps may store data locally on your device (such as preferences, settings, or progress) to improve functionality. This data:
  • Remains stored only on your device
  • Is not transmitted to any server
  • Is not accessed by the developer
  • Is deleted when you uninstall the App or reset the data within the App

Internet Access

  • Our Apps are designed to work offline whenever possible. If an App includes optional internet features, they are used only for functionality purposes and do not involve collecting personal data.

Advertising

  • Our Apps do not display advertisements unless clearly stated in the specific App listing.

Analytics

  • Our Apps do not use analytics tools, tracking systems, or behavior monitoring services.

Permissions

  • Our Apps do not request unnecessary permissions. If any permission is required, it is only used for core functionality and not for collecting personal data.

Third-Party Services

  • Our Apps do not integrate third-party services that collect personal information.

Children's Privacy

  • Our Apps do not knowingly collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

Legal Disclaimer

  • Content provided in the Apps is for informational and organizational purposes only and does not constitute legal, financial, or professional advice. Users should consult a qualified professional for specific advice regarding their situation.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

    • We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by updating the “Last Updated” date above.
